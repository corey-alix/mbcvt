Directions

 

We're located on Route 100 in the Village of Westfield with easy access from I-91.

 

From Boston, MA and Points South

We’re just a three and a half hour drive from Boston, MA.

 

Take ramp left for I-93 North toward Concord NH

From I-93 North enter Vermont, Take ramp right for I-91 North Toward St. Johnsbury.

From I-91 in Vermont, at exit 26, take ramp right for VT-58/US-5 towards Orleans, VT

Turn right onto US-5/VT-58/Route 5

Keep Straight onto US-5/Route 5

Turn left onto VT-14

Keep Straight onto VT-100

Arrive at 1184 VT-100, Westfield VT 05874. If you drive past Carmel Road, you’ve gone too far.

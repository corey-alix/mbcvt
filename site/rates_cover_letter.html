Dear 2024 Campers, I hope this message finds you well and eagerly anticipating another wonderful camping season at Millbrook Campground. In anticipation of state and local tax increases and inflation in general, I must share this burden with you by increasing rates. Effective for the 2025 camping season, the seasonal rate will be $1950 per site. To help offset this increase, I am offering a $100 discount for those who pay in full before October. The seasonal cost will be $1850 per site if paid in full before October 1st, 2024. For campers that wish to leave their camper on site over the winter, the cost will be $300 for the winter and that will be applied to your 2025 seasonal rate. The winter storage fee will be due by October 1st, 2024. I understand that change requires reflection, but I assure you that this adjustment will prove necessary to sustain the high quality experience you expect from Millbrook Campground. I am committed to providing you with a beautiful, memorable and peaceful experience. Thank you for your understanding and continued support and I want to seeing each and every one of you back here for a fantastic 2025.

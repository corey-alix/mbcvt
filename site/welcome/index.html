We're in Northern Vermont

Located in northeastern Vermont, we're open from May through September and longer if weather permits.

We accommodate RV's and tents with 30 campsites nestled along a stream. All RV sites have full hookups with water, sewer and electric. All our tent sites also have water and electric.

The Millbrook Campground has direct access to local ATV Trails. Come on up and ride! Also, enjoy local hike and bike trails or area lakes during your stay. We are also near the Jay Peak Resort.

Just 90 minutes from Montreal, 3.5 hours from Boston, and 4.5 hours from Albany, NY

What's At the Millbrook Campground?

Our Amenities

We are a campground that caters to the whole family. Most campsites have frontage along a local brook, surrounded by forests. A natural pond and local stream are a part of the Millbrook Campground and available for you and your family to explore and enjoy.

  • Free Showers
  • Free Wifi
  • Full Hook-Ups
  • Natural Pond, Stream and Forest
  • Play Area & Rec Hall
  • Walk to Shopping
  • ATV Trail Access

For the youngin's we have available landscaped lawns for family fun..

From the Millbrook Campground, you're within walking distance to the Westfield General Store for general merchandise, groceries and sundries.

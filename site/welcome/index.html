Location

We are located in the North East Kingdom of Vermont, the most lovely and approchable place in the world. Listen to the birds sing, the brook babble, and the crackle of the fire as the sun sets, the moon rises and you begin to end another perfect day in Vermont.

Season

Utilities

RV sites have full hookups with water, sewer and electric. Tent sites have water and electric.

Recreation

The Millbrook Campground has direct access to local ATV Trails. We are also near the northern terminus of the Long Trail, the Jay Peak Ski Area, and the Jay Recreation Center. Adventure is literally just around the corner!

Amenities

Most sites have frontage along a local brook. The campground is nestled against the forest and we have a clean pond and local stream for you and your family to explore and enjoy.

  • Free showers
  • Free wifi
  • Full hook-ups
  • Seasoned Firewood
  • Play Area
  • Rec Hall (under construction)
  • Natural Pond, Stream and Forest
  • ATV Trail Access

Shopping

The Millbrook Campground share both the Westfield General Store and Home Town Hardware as close neighbors.

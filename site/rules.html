Campground Rules

Please note the following rules

Check-in is at 2:00 PM. Please do not come in earlier unless you have made arrangements with the office. Visitors are $2.00 per person. You are responsible for your visitors, so make sure they stop at the office to sign-in and pay before they continue into the campground. If they do not it will be added to your bill. Visitors must stop and sign-in and there are No Exceptions. Visitor Vehicles must be left in the designated parking area unless other arrangements have been made with the management. Overnight Visitors are $5.00 per person, please pay at the office prior to their stay. Luxury Utilities are $5.00 per day. This includes air conditioners, electric heaters, etc. Pay in advance if you choose to use them, this includes seasonal guests. Clothes washers and outdoor refrigerators are not permitted. Quite time is 10:00 PM. Please be considerate of your neighbors. Dogs must be on a leash at all times and dropping must be cleaned immediately. Owners are responsible for there pets. Do not bring aggressive or loud animals into the campground. Trees and Fireplaces must not be altered without permission. Children must be attended to in the restrooms, playground, brook or pond. Bath/Swim in the brook or pond at your own risk. Trash may be placed in the dumpster located near the exit. Personal Property is not the responsibility of Millbrook Campground. Check-out is at 1:00 PM. If you would like to stay longer you may check with the office. A fee of $5.00 per hour will be charged. The WiFi password for Millbrook Guest is millbrook2019.
